Recipe of Chicken Nuggets

Description

These chicken nuggets, made from scratch from boneless chicken thighs and breasts, are so much more than your average chicken nuggets , so good that you might end up calling them bang bang boom boom chicken! Use any extra of the spicy,sweet, tangy bang bang sauce as a dip or sandwich spread, it is very delicious.

Ingredients for Nuggets

  • 1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breasts and thighs
  • 2 tablespoons fine dry breadcrumbs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly groundblack pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup self-rising flour
  • 1/3 cup sliced green onions
  • 3 cups vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

Ingredients for Sauce

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3 tablespoons sriracha hot sauce, plus more for serving
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 3 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground sichuan pepper (optional)

Cooking Process

Step 1

Cut chicken into about 1 to 1 1/2 inch pieces;add to the bowl of a food processor. Add breadcrumbs, salt, paprica, black pepper, cayenne, garlic powder, and the 1 tablespoon vegetable oil to the food processor, and pulse on and off until mixture is finely ground, but not yet a smooth puree

Step 2

Scoop 2 tablespoon-sized portions onto a sheet pan, making 24 total. cover and chill for about 30 minutes. meanwhile, shake self-risisng flour and cornstarch together in a bag, and set aside (if using all-purpose flour, add in 1/2 teaspoon baking soda).

Step 3

Using slightly damp hands, shape each portion into a ball;add 5 or 6 at a time to the flour mixture in the bag. Toss to coat, shake off excess, and place back on the sheet pan. once coated, form each into the nugget shape of your choice.\ A ball shape works well for deep frying; if pan-frying, a flat nugget works best.

Step 4

Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 to 2 hours, or until ready to fry. Nuggets can be made aday ahead.

Step 5

While nuggets chill, whisk mayonnaise, honey, sriracha, soy sause, seasame oil, rice vinegar and sichuan pepper together in a bowl; cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Step 6

Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C) Deep fry nuggets until no longer pink at the center and golden brown on the outside, about 4 minutes, An instant read the thermometer inserted near the center shoul read 165 degrees F(74 degrees C). Alternately, heat oil as needed in a skillet over medium-high heat, and shallow fry until cooked as above, 2 to 3 minutes per sisde.

Step 7

Transfer cooked nuggets to a mixing bowl, and add the sliced green onions. pour in about 1/2 cup sauce, and toss to coat. Extra sauce can be served alongside, or used for other applications , as sandwich spread or as a dip.

Step 8

Transfer to a serving platter, top with more sriracha and remaining green onions. Serve and enjoy the delicious chicken nuggets
